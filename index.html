About Me

Hi There

I work "in the overlap of the Venn diagram of design & development" to quote my own Twitter profile. I've worked in the digital ad space for a number of years, creating mostly simple, straightforward ads. But as users become ever more "banner blind", we have to be more creative in gaining their attention without becoming obnoxious, and without drawing the ire of a Chrome Heavy intervention. Interactive ads are really, functionally, little single-purpose websites on somebody else's website page. I am currently strengthening my developer skills at Eleven Fifty Academy to create interactive experiences small and large.
